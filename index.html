FAQ

Short answers below. The official rulebook and updates stay on Discord.

What is AevonSMP?
A Philippine-based Minecraft community built on peace, order, and respect: structured rules, fair play, and a mature, non-toxic environment for Java and Bedrock.
How do I join on Java vs Bedrock?
Use hostname aevonsmp.online. On Bedrock, set port 19023. If the hostname fails, use the numeric IP from the Connect section with the same port for your edition.
Is griefing or stealing allowed?
No. Griefing, stealing, trapping, and damaging community areas are against the rules. Unclaimed builds are not guaranteed protection, so use claims and secure your items.
Can I use mods, macros, or “quality of life” cheats?
Cheating of any kind is forbidden, including unauthorized clients, X-Ray, duplication glitches, exploits, and macros that give unfair advantages. When in doubt, ask staff before using a mod.
How do I apply for staff or streamer / media?
We recruit dedicated people who can help keep the community safe and positive. Use the Staff section below for application graphics and check Discord for open forms or announcements.
Where are the full rules?
The written rulebook covers Minecraft and Discord: conduct, gameplay, communication, staff authority, and privacy. Join Discord for the latest version and help from staff.
